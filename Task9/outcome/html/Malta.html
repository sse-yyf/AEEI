Summary of cost of living in Malta:

  • A family of four estimated monthly costs are 3,035.2$ (2,842.2€) without rent.

  • Cost of living in Malta is, on average, 4.5% lower than in Sweden.
  • Rent in Malta is, on average, 26.1% higher than in Sweden.
